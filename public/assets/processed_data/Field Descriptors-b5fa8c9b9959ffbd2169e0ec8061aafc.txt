Name
	- Name of the point of interest (required)
Description
	- Short description of the POI
Category
	- Category (e.g. School, Airport, Sea Port) (required)
Lat-Deg
	- Degree component of latitude (required)
Lat-Min
	- Minute component of latitude (defaults to 0)
Lat-Sec
	- Second component of latitude (defaults to 0)
Lat-Dir
	- Direction component of latitude (defaults to N)
Lon-Deg
	- Degree component of longitude (required)
Lon-Min
	- Minute component of longitude (defaults to 0)
Lon-Sec
	- Second component of longitude (defaults to 0)
Lon-Dir
	- Direction component of longitude (defaults to E)
